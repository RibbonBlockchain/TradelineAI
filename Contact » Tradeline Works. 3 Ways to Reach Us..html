Buyers FAQ

What is a tradeline?

A tradeline is a credit account that shows up on your credit report when you apply for any form of debt, e.g., credit card, car loan, student loan, personal loan, line of credit, or a mortgage.

What is the benefit of purchasing a tradeline?

When you buy a tradeline, you will be added as an authorized user to the seller’s account. Once the credit account shows up on your report, you’ll benefit by getting the credit age, and the limit added to your report saving you years that it may have taken you to get the same result. Buying tradelines is also known in the industry as piggybacking credit.

Do you have a money back guarantee?

Yes, we have a money-back guarantee! If the tradeline(s) you purchased did not report to at least 1 or more of the credit bureau companies by the end of the initial three months period, you get your money back, no questions asked.

Sellers FAQ

Is it legal to sell tradelines?

The practice of selling tradelines is legal; however, there have been many cases of abuse and illegal practices in the tradeline industry. At Tradeline Works, we are committed to operating ethically, acting on the highest professional standards, and complying with all relevant laws and regulations.

Are tradelines worth it?

We think it is! And over 100 sellers agree that selling tradelines is viable and the simplest way to earn passive income online. It usually takes less than 5 minutes to add someone as an authorized user to your account, and we’re certain it’s the easiest money you’ll ever earn.

How much money can you make selling tradelines?

The amount you can earn depends on your credit card age and credit limit. The older your credit card age and the higher your limit, the more money you’ll make since we pay you a percentage of the sales price. However, most sellers earn between $80 to $370 per authorized user.
